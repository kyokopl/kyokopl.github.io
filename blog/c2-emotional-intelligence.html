What learnings did you take from the chapter?

I took away the importance of the following 5 factors: Self-awareness, Self-regulation, Motivation, Empathy, and Social skills. These are all learnt attribute that will enable an individual to really succeed in all aspects of life.

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

I found it very interesting as it resonated with my son and I at bedtime, often when he wants me to lie down with him until he sleeps, this time is fully encompassed by mindfulness. It also reminded me of a blog I recently read on Raised Good. She now seeks solace in this quiet mindfulness as such.

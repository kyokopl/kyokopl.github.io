Self Confidence

02 July 2018

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

They are the same. Having self awareness means you must also have mindfulness. They are both the ability to stay grounded when emotions can get high.

Self awareness practice, Body Scan. What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

I felt great, I noticed I was able to forget about a lot of the small things I was thinking about at the time. It was also quite interesting how long 1 or 2 minutes could be. I think this practoce would work best for me, and the next time my emotions are hightened, I definitely want to give it a try.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

I took away the concept itself. I have always known my emotions are not me, however, I have never viewed it quite this way before. This being the way we can manage them, as we would a sore arm. I have control over these emotions and there are ways to manage them, such as going for a walk, choosing to not let an emotion take cotrol of me, and who I am.
